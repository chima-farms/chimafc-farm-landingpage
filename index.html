About Us

Welcome to Chima FC Farms, a family-owned and operated farm dedicated to raising healthy and sustainable poultry, fish, and snails. Our story began with a passion to nourish our community with fresh, locally raised products, and a commitment to environmentally conscious farming practices. Founded in January 2013 by Dr. Favour Chima, our farm has grown into a trusted source of wholesome food, rooted in tradition and driven by innovation. We're proud to provide our community with access to nutritious, humanely raised meat products.

At Chima FC Farm, our mission is to provide consumers with fresh, healthy, and delicious poultry, fish, and snail products while maintaining a commitment to ethical and humane farming practices.

Our vision is to become the number one choice for locally raised, organic poultry, fish, and snails for every Nigerian home. We envision a future where our agricultural practices set a gold standard for sustainability, innovation, and nourishment, inspiring healthier communities and a greener planet.

Services

Our Healthy Services

Sustainable Livestock Production

At Chima FC Farm, we prioritize animal welfare and environmental sustainability. Our livestock farming services include:

  • Raising healthy chickens, fish, and snails in a clean and hygienic environment
  • Feeding our animals with organic, non-GMO food to ensure optimal nutrition and flavor
  • Implementing best practices in animal husbandry to promote animal health and well-being
Humane Meat Processing

We take pride in our on-farm meat processing facility, where we:

  • Humanely butcher and process our livestock to ensure the highest quality meat
  • Follow strict food safety guidelines to prevent contamination and ensure a safe product
  • Store our meat products in a clean, temperature-controlled environment to maintain freshness and quality
Fresh Sales and Delivery

Get the freshest meat products delivered right to your doorstep! Our sales services include:

  • Direct sales of our farm-raised meat products
  • Competitive pricing and flexible packaging options
  • Convenient delivery or on-farm pickup options
  • Friendly customer service and support
